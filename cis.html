Introduction

Through and through, I am a designer. I am known by everyone as the "...what if we tried it like this" guy, and for the most part, when I propose such an idea, it sticks. My design roles over the years have manifested in many ways, however, nothing has captivated my interest as much as web development. My disposition is to spend my time immersed in front-end systems, obsessing over the intricacies of responsive design, ensuring the most seamless and intuitive user experience possible. My current muse is studying various animation platforms such as GreenSock. I am also passionate about taking on back-end challenges, and thoroughly enjoy the satisfaction of properly organized and documented code. I am endlessly excited by the learning opportunities within this industry and look forward to the collective future that is being built within the world of code.
